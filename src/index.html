Our Services

What's Included?

At Blades Landscaping & Lawn Care we offer a wide range of services to keep your lawn looking its best. We offer everything from lawn mowing to full landscape design. We're a full-service lawn care company, and can handle any job, big or small.

  • Lawn Mowing

    Proper mowing doesn't only give your lawn a clean look, it also helps keep it healthy. We'll make sure your lawn is mowed to the proper height and frequency to keep it looking its best.

  • Seeding & Fertilization

    Spot treat bare areas or overseed your whole lawn. We can also spread fertilizer to help keep your lawn healthy and green.

  • Weed & Pest Control

    We can keep weeds and bugs from taking over your lawn. We offer family-friendly treatments to keep your lawn healthy and pest-free.

  • Aeration

    Puncturing the soild with small holes in order to let water, air, and nutrients penetrate the grass roots. This helps the roots grow deeply and produce a stronger, more vigorous lawn.

  • Snow Removal

    We can remove snow from your driveway and walkways. We'll make sure your property is safe and accessible. We can also spread salt to help prevent ice from forming.

  • Gutter Cleaning

    Cleaning out leaves and debris from your gutters is an important part of maintaining your home. We'll make sure your gutters are clean and funtioning properly.
